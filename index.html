Web Development

I am a web developer: each day I have a fresh set of puzzles and learning opportunities and I will solve these problems through collaborating with teammates. It's in programming I found my ideal and energizing work environment. I am an innovative and original thinker that values the insights of others.

I currently attend Epicodus--a six-month full-time programming school for novice web developers. My courses include .Net Core, Ember, Angular, C#, SQL, Javascript, CSS, and HTML. Programming, however, was a hobby before a career. Beyond programming, I am applying to business schools throughout 2016-2017 as I continue to pursue entrepreneurship opportunities. If it's rain and I have free time, I play chess. If it's sunny, I enjoy climbing, rafting, skiing, and site seeing.
